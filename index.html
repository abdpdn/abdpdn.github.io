Welcome to my academic website.

I am a postdoctoral researcher at the CNRS–Laboratoire de Physique Théorique (LPT), Toulouse, working with Dr. Nicolas Laflorencie. My research focuses on strongly correlated quantum many-body systems, topological phases, many-body localization, quasiperiodic systems, and non-Hermitian quantum matter.
