Welcome to my academic website.

I am a postdoctoral researcher in theoretical condensed matter physics working with Dr. Nicolas Laflorencie at CNRS–LPT Toulouse. My research focuses on strongly correlated quantum many-body systems, topological phases of matter, many-body localization, quasiperiodic systems, and non-Hermitian physics.

This website contains my research interests, publications, software, conference talks, curriculum vitae, and contact information.
